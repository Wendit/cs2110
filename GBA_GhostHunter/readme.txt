Aim the ghosts and shoot. 

Use the arrow keys to move the aim and press A to shoot.

While pressing B, the moving speed of the aim will slow down.

Each enemy falls out the bottom of the screen will decrease the game's remaining time.

Ghosts wearing pumpuking helms can take more than one hits.

If the player hits an innocent, the score will go down by 10.

By hitting the floating boost item, player will enter fever time.The score factor will be doubled each time. The fever time can be extended. When fever time ends, the score factor goes back to 1.
